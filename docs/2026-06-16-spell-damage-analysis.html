Arkanoid RPG — Spell Block-Damage Analysis

Generated 2026-06-16 · all spells, ranked by block-HP a single cast can damage on its own. PRE-BALANCE baseline — this is the snapshot that motivated the 2026-06-16 balance pass (Turret/Phoenix/Last Day buffed, Fire Wall toned 3→2, Lightning/Overload/Tesla/Containment/Golem widened). Numbers below are the before; regenerate after playtesting if you want post-balance figures.

How to read this. Numbers are estimates at spell level 1 on a dense ~12-wide board, assuming best-case positioning (the most a single cast can do alone, no relics/cards). "Block-HP damaged" = total hit-points removed across all blocks, so a spell that chips 10 blocks for 1 = 10, same as one block for 10. "Self / mana" is that number divided by mana cost (efficiency). Real clears vary with the board, ball position, spell level, hero stats, relics and cards — treat this as a relative ranking, not exact damage. Sorted by Self block-HP (descending), as requested.

Takeaways
• Best solo clears: Fire Wall (board-clear when cast low), then the Paladin/Engineer field spells (Reckoning, Last Day, Containment, Tesla).
• Best HP-per-mana: Reckoning (2.4) and Containment (1.8) — but Reckoning is gated behind taking damage.
• Highest ceiling, period: the Ignite → Conflagration chain (optionally + Fire Wall / Ashfall) — set the whole board alight, then detonate it all at 6 each for the biggest single burst in the game.
• Force-multipliers (Lich's Gaze curse, Duplicate/Raise, Penetration, Decay) read as "0 damage" here but can double or more the effective output of every ball-based clear.
• Weakest for cost: Phoenix and Turret (low HP/mana for 70 / 30).
